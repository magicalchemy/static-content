# Chapter One: Mendelef`s Legacy

Since the end of the War with the Mad God and up to the present day, the goblin people have passed down the knowledge of the Great Prophecy. It first appeared in a vision to the High Priestess of the Elves on the night after the final battle with Adomion.

The prophecy said: *“The Great Evil shall return to this world, and only the first generation of Mendellef’s children is destined to stop it.”*

For long centuries, the goblins kept a careful faith in these words. For hundreds and thousands of years, they lived guided by the prophecy, but time is merciless. It erases cities, and it erases the memory in people’s hearts. Generations came and went, and for many, the prophecy became nothing more than one of the beautiful legends of the Golden Age.

Once, every goblin knew and believed in what had been revealed to the priestess, but now only a few continue to seek its true meaning.

The great alchemists of past ages tried in vain to unravel the prophecy’s secret, to find the key to its fulfillment. They remembered: the first generation of goblins, born by Mendelef himself, had lived at the dawn of the Golden Age. But that age had vanished many thousands of years ago, and no one could understand how, in our time, the children of that first generation could possibly appear.

And yet, for centuries, the goblins continued their search. With special care, they gathered everything that might be connected to the time when the great masters of alchemy lived and created in Dji`Da.

Hundreds of ancient artifacts were collected: scrolls and books in forgotten tongues, magical crystals, and relics whose purpose had long been swallowed by the abyss of time.

But the most mysterious finds were the ancient flasks, each containing a strange, glowing substance. Every flask was unique; no two were ever the same. Neither magic nor brute force could open them, and the nature of their contents remained beyond the understanding of even the wisest alchemists.

One thing, however, was beyond doubt: these flasks were the oldest relics ever discovered. And perhaps they had been created in that very age when the children of Mendelef first appeared.

## Storyboard for a promotional video

**First Frame: A panoramic shot opens onto the ancient capital of the goblins. Some buildings are partially ruined, and the spires of several towers have collapsed. The city itself lies in a vast valley, surrounded by towering, snow-capped peaks.**

*Voiceover: "For many thousands of years, the goblin race was divided, wandering all the roads of Dji`Da."*

**Second Frame: A view opens onto a massive central square. A crowd of goblins from all four tribes stands there, all eyes fixed on the center of the square. The goblins are dressed according to their respective tribes. Numerous carts and wagons are visible on the streets adjacent to the square.**

**In the very center of the square, an altar-cauldron is installed. In the background, a chain of snow-covered mountains stands tall.**

*Voiceover: "And finally, it has happened. We have found our lost Homeland, and the people of Mendelef are whole once more."*

**Third Frame: A close-up shot of the center of the square.
Four High Alchemists - one from each tribe - are in the frame. The distinctions between their tribes must be clearly visible in their attire. They slowly approach the altar-cauldron and each pours their own vial of potion into it.**

**The altar begins to glow with a vibrant green light. Thin, luminous lines start to spread from it across the ground, drawing a magical pattern that ripples through the entire capital.**

*Voiceover: "The Great Evil will come to this world once again, and only the first generation of Mendelef's children is destined to stop it."*

**Fourth Frame: Runes and patterns light up on the facades of the ruined buildings, and fountains along the streets begin to flow. Magical crystals flare to life inside the street lanterns.**

**In the distance, among the mountains, a waterfall begins to cascade, and a watermill slowly turns its wheels.**

*Voiceover: "So states our Great Prophecy. And thanks to the ancient lore we discovered, we have found the key to unlocking the truth hidden within these words."*

**Fifth Frame: A shot focusing on Mendelef's Flask. Its contents are obscured at first, filled with a swirling mist. Gradually, the fog clears, revealing the silhouette of an infant inside. At the very end of the video, the baby opens its eyes.**

*Voiceover: "Mendelef's Flasks, carefully gathered by us for generations, contained the very seed of life."*

*"And after millennia, the life breathed into being by our divine father himself, Tara Mendelef, has returned to the world of Dji`Da..."*

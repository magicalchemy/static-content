# The Invasion of the Orcs from the Southern Tribes and Its Consequences
## Continent Jarah

Jarah is a largely unexplored southern continent. It is believed that most of its land is covered by the vast, barren desert known as Nong Chaum, which translates to "Dead Land." Scholars from the Bizaria Academy suggest that this desert, much like the Twilight Wastes, was formed as a result of a magical catastrophe long before the New Era.

Few travelers who have attempted to cross the desert have returned, and most of them vanished without a trace, swallowed by the sun-baked dunes. Seafarers who sailed along Jara’s coasts, as far as their supplies and water allowed, reported nothing but endless, lifeless sands stretching to the horizon.
## The Southern Tribes
The Southern Tribes are descendants of the orcs who were defeated by the alliance of humans, dwarves, and elves during the Wars of the Survivors. The remnants of the orcish army fled to the continent of Jara over three thousand years ago. It was believed that they perished in this harsh and inhospitable land.

However, the arrival of a massive orc fleet on the southern shores of the Dujun Empire took everyone by surprise. Coastal city garrisons desperately tried to hold off the invading armada but were no match for the thousands-strong horde of bloodthirsty orcs.

In a short time, the coastal cities and settlements were overrun. Those who managed to escape could still hear the dying screams of their fellow citizens in their nightmares for many days.

This orc invasion was unlike any previous raids by their northern kin. This was not just a plundering expedition or a slave-taking raid; it was a full-scale military invasion with the intention of conquering land.

The orcs began establishing their settlements on the captured territories, and more waves of orcs kept arriving by sea. According to the reports from scouts, they were not only transporting soldiers but also women and children.
## The Dudzhun Empire
For the Dudzhun Empire, the orc invasion was a complete shock. There had never been any mention in Dudzhun history of an orc threat from the southern continent of Jarah. No one could have predicted that disaster would come from there.

The orcs had always been little more than bogeymen for children and a problem only for the faraway western continent of Farnax. Common folk in the Empire had only heard of orcs from travelers and merchants. The Empire lost much of its southern provinces in the war against the orcs. Emperor Leo Paul VII continuously tried to reclaim the lost territories, but the Empire’s strength was only enough to defend against further orc attacks and prevent the central provinces from falling.

## The Theocracy of Leria
During the orc invasion of the Dudzhun Empire, the Holy Throne sent emissaries to convert the orcs to the Holy Faith.

However, after the knights of the Church of the Sun God were brutally attacked and their camp set on fire, and the head of Bishop Mityr was delivered to the capital of Belstal, the idea of converting the orcs into followers of the Holy Church quickly evaporated.

The orcs were declared heretics, and the Lord Pontiff of the Holy Throne blessed the White Legion of the Church to embark on a holy crusade against the enemies of faith.
## The Invia Alliance
Many free companies joined the war against the orcs of the Southern Tribes.

The Golden Lions, Thousand Swords, and Mountain Swallows were among the most famous mercenary companies hired by the Dujun Empire to defend its lands. It is rumored that the gold spent on these and other mercenary companies could have supported a large city for five years.
## The Emirate of Eastern Balkh 
Due to the new threat of the orcs from the Southern Tribes, the Emir of Eastern Balkh stopped his ongoing war with the Balkh Khanate and declared a fragile truce. He sent most of his forces from the northern borders to reinforce the south. These troops were to defend the territories of Eastern Balkh from the orcs.

However, in reality, the Emir is considering taking advantage of his southern neighbor's weakness and seize disputed territories from the Dudzhun Empire. If the Empire begins losing to the orcs, it will have no strength left to reclaim the lost lands.
## The Balkh Khanate
The Khan of Balkh was not idle either. Thanks to the truce with the Emirate of Eastern Balkh, he fully concentrated his efforts on preparing for a military campaign against Aferia, as now was the perfect opportunity to capture the lands of his old enemy.

Many mercenary companies, drawn by the gold of the Empire, made agreements with it and thus could not be hired by Aferia.

“The day has come when the scales of our long-standing conflict can finally tip in our favor,” thought Khan Osram III.


**The continent of Khela was experiencing turbulent times. All the nations watched closely as events unfolded in the Dudzhun Empire. The steps it would take would determine whether the Dudzhun Empire would stand or fall.**
